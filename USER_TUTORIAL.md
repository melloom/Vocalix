## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is Echo Garden free?**
A: Yes! Echo Garden is completely free to use.

**Q: Do I need to use my real name?**
A: No! Echo Garden is designed for anonymous or pseudonymous sharing. Use any handle you want.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, you can delete your account in Settings. This removes your profile and clips.

**Q: How long can my clips be?**
A: Regular clips are 30 seconds. Podcast mode allows up to 10 minutes.

**Q: Can I edit my clips after publishing?**
A: Currently, you can't edit clips after publishing. You can delete and re-record if needed.

### Recording Questions

**Q: Why isn't my microphone working?**
A: 
- Check browser permissions (allow microphone access)
- Make sure you're on HTTPS (required for recording)
- Try a different browser
- Check your microphone settings

**Q: Can I record on mobile?**
A: Yes! Echo Garden works on mobile browsers. Make sure to allow microphone permissions.

**Q: What audio format is used?**
A: Clips are recorded as WebM/Opus and converted to MP3 for storage.

**Q: Can I upload pre-recorded audio?**
A: Currently, you can only record directly in the browser. Upload feature may come later.

### Content Questions

**Q: How does moderation work?**
A: 
- AI automatically checks clips
- Human moderators review flagged content
- You can report inappropriate content
- Violations may result in content removal or account suspension

**Q: Can I use copyrighted music?**
A: No. Only use original content or content you have rights to. Copyright violations may result in removal.

**Q: What happens if my clip is removed?**
A: You'll be notified if your clip is removed. You can appeal if you believe it was a mistake.

**Q: Can I make my clips private?**
A: Currently, all clips are public. Private clips may be added in the future.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why are my clips taking so long to process?**
A: Processing includes transcription, summarization, and moderation. This usually takes 10-30 seconds. High traffic may cause delays.

**Q: Can I download clips?**
A: Currently, you can't download clips. This feature may be added later.

**Q: Do clips expire?**
A: No, clips stay on Echo Garden indefinitely unless you delete them or they're removed for violations.

**Q: Can I see who listened to my clips?**
A: You can see listen counts, but not individual listeners (privacy feature).

### Community Questions

**Q: How do I report harassment?**
A: Use the report button on any clip or profile. Our moderation team will review it.

**Q: Can I block users?**
A: Yes! You can block users from their profile page. Click the three-dot menu (â‹®) next to the Follow button and select "Block User". Blocked users won't be able to see your clips or interact with you, and you won't see their content. You can manage blocked users in Settings.

**Q: How do communities work?**
A: Communities are groups around topics or interests. Join to see community-specific content and post to communities.

**Q: Can I moderate a community?**
A: Community creators can set moderators. Contact the community creator if you're interested.
